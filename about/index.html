About SACSAB

Swedish Airspace Control Systems AB (SACSAB) is a high‑tech defense start-up based in Uppsala, specialising in autonomous airspace monitoring and control solutions. Our mission is to provide reliable, secure, and safe systems that help organisations regain control over increasingly complex airspace environments.

We combine advanced sensing technologies, autonomous decision‑making, and robust system engineering to deliver solutions capable of operating in demanding, safety‑critical contexts.
