About SACSAB

Swedish Airspace Control Systems AB (SACSAB) is a high‑tech defense start-up based in Uppsala, specialising in autonomous airspace monitoring and control solutions. Our mission is to provide reliable, secure, and safe systems that help organisations regain control over increasingly complex airspace environments.

SACSAB was founded 2026 in Uppsala, Sweden, by five civil engineering students specialising in different areas like embedded systems, data analysis, and computer systems.

This page is, like SACSAB, in a really early start-up phase. As such, this website will be expanded with our history, mission, and leadership in the near future.
